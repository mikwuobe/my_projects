Chin Chin

Description

Chin Chin is made of dough containing flour,sugar,butter and milk

How to Prepare

Step 1: In a small bowl, whisk together
milk and vanilla extract until well combined and set aside
Step 2: In a large bowl, whisk together flour, sugar
Step 3: On a lightl floured work surface, divide dough into 4 equal portions
Step 4: Line a large strainer with paper towels
Step 5: Serve hot and enjoy!
