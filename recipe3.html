Pepper Soup

Description

Pepper Soup is a popular Nigerian one-pot dish made with meat, pepper, onions, and a blend of spices.

How to Prepare

Step 1: Prepare the meat: Wash and cut your preferred meat (chicken, goat, etc.)
into desired pieces.
Step 2: Marinate: Marinate the meat with a blend of pepper (fresh or pre-ground),
spices (thyme, bay leaf, etc.), and other desired ingredients like ginger, garlic, and seasoning cubes.
Step 3: Cook: Boil the marinated meat with water, chopped onions, and stock (optional).
Step 4: Simmer: Reduce heat and simmer until the meat is tender and cooked through.
Step 5: Adjust: Add additional seasoning or pepper to taste before serving.
