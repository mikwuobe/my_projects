Efo Riro

Description

Jollof rice is a popular Nigerian one-pot dish made with rice, tomatoes, onions, and a blend of spices.

How to Prepare

Step 1: Wash and chop vegetables: Clean and chop spinach (or other leafy green vegetable),
tomatoes, onions, and desired protein (meat, fish, etc.)
Step 2: Parboil meat/fish (optional): If using meat or fish, parboil it separately until slightly cooked.
Step 3: Add rice and water
Heat palm oil and fry chopped onions until translucent.
Step 4: Add ingredients: Add blended or chopped tomatoes, pepper (fresh or pre-ground),
and other desired spices (thyme, bay leaf, etc.).
Step 5: Cook: Allow the sauce to simmer for a few minutes.
Step 6: Add vegetables: Add the leafy greens and any pre-cooked protein.
Step 7: Season: Season with salt and stock (optional) to taste.
Step 8: Simmer: Simmer until the vegetables are wilted, and the flavors are combined.
