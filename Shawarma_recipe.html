Description

This recipe brings a taste of Shawarma Paradise to your home kitchen. It captures the essence of
our signature shawarma with readily available ingredients and a simplified cooking method.

How to Prepare

Step 1: Marinate the meat: In a large bowl, combine yogurt, lemon juice, olive oil, spices (cumin,
coriander, paprika, garlic powder, onion powder, cinnamon), salt, and pepper. Add chicken
breasts (or lamb, beef) and toss to coat evenly. Cover and refrigerate for at least 30 minutes,
or up to overnight for deeper flavor.
Step 2: Cook the meat: Preheat oven to 400°F (200°C). Arrange chicken pieces on a baking sheet
and bake for 20-25 minutes, or until cooked through and juices run clear. Alternatively, grill the
chicken for a smoky flavor.
Step 3: Prepare the vegetables: While the meat cooks, chop tomatoes, cucumber, red onion, and parsley.
Step 4: Make the sauce: In a small bowl, whisk together mayonnaise, yogurt, tahini (if
using), lemon juice, garlic, salt, and pepper. Adjust seasonings to taste.
Step 5: Assemble the shawarma: Warm pita breads in the oven or microwave. Slice the cooked
chicken into thin strips. Spread desired amount of sauce inside each pita bread. Fill with
chicken strips, tomatoes, cucumber, red onion, and parsley. Sprinkle with dried oregano for
added flavor.
Step 6: Enjoy! Roll up the pita bread and savor the delicious taste of Pinch of Heaven Shawarma at home.
