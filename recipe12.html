Moi Moi

Description

Moi moi is a steamed or boiled beans pudding made from a mixture of washed
and peeled beans and onoins, fresh red peppers, spices and cryfish.

How to Prepare

Step 1: Soak the beans
Step 2: Peel your beans
Step 3: Sieve the skin of the beans out
Step 4: Blend the beans and other ingredients
Step 5: Serve hot and enjoy!
